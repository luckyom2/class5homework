IMFORMATION ABOUT CATS

Cats are domestic animals. They are small in size. Their bodies are covered with smooth fur. They have two mesmerizing eyes, two highly sensitive ears, four legs, whiskers under their nose and a long tail. Cats are of three kinds, namely- farm cats, house cats and feral cats.

Cats were domesticated around 9,000 years ago in the Near East. They likely began living alongside humans to help control rodent populations in agricultural settings.
